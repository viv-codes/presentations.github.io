How are supercomputers different from my laptop?

Scale

Solving advanced computational problems and simulations requires immense parallel processing power, much greater than any single system.

Cost

Frontier here cost Oak Ridge National Lab $600million. At Los Alamos, we just brought in a set of systems totalling around a half million dollars.

Infrastructure

In addition to having cutting edge, complex nodes, supercomputers require cutting edge facilities. At LANL, our main computing complex uses more power than both surrounding towns. Frontier here uses more power than all of RIT, including our HPC center.

Cooling

In addition to power, these systems require absolutely massive cooling systems. In the floor under these systems are 3-6 foot pipes bringing thousands of gallons of water to and from cooling towers in order to keep these systems cool and running.

Hardware

Due to the high demands of the simulation codes run on HPC machines, many cutting edge supercomputers will use technologies that aren't avaliable to the general market. Grace hopper / venado / ATS
